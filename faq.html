Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join CS club?

Join our Google Classroom (class code: 6bzuaye, make sure you’re using gapps), follow us on Instagram @rhhs.cs.club for updates, and join our Discord!

I’m a beginner/don’t know how to code, can I still join CS club?

Yes! We have a group for competitive programming targeted towards beginners. We also have a web group open to beginners where you learn how to make a website!

When and where is CS club?

We meet Thursdays in the library after school!

Who should join CS club? Is it right for me?

If you have an interest in CS and want to meet like minded people, expand your knowledge, or do anything computer science related, join!

What do we do in CS club?

CS club has 3 groups and what each group does is different. Read more about each group to find out!

Can I participate in the Canadian Computing Contest through CS club?

Yes, we plan to host the CCC every year around February! Stay tuned for updates!

Are there any resources used in CS club? Do I need to install anything beforehand?

In most cases, probably not. Check with an exec running your group or take a look at previous lessons to see if there’s anything that needs to be installed for the next lesson.

Will the things I learn in CS club help me in the actual computer science courses offered at RHHS?

Unfortunately, we do not teach material that is taught in the RHHS computer science courses; however, many computer science concepts are similar across different programming languages therefore the critical thinking skills you can pick up at CS club can be applied everywhere. We’re also available for general CS homework help.

Don’t See Your Question?

Email us at rhhs.compsci@gmail.com, DM us @rhhs.cs.club or message one of the execs. If your question is common enough, we’ll add it here too!
