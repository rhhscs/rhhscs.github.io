## Intro
This group was created for students who are interested in computer science, but have little or no experience. Students will learn a new concept using JavaScript every week, along with working through a variety of activities designed to improve their skills in computer science.

## Contest
This group was created for students who are looking to prepare for the upcoming Canadian Computing Contest held by the University of Waterloo in February. Students will focus on building specific skills and techniques that will help them succeed in contest-based questions.

## Web
This group is perfect for students who have an adequate amount of experience in their programming skills, such as those who have finished the intro group, or completed any computer science course (e.g. ICS2O, ICS3U). Students will learn more advanced concepts with experienced executives and will be familiar with designing websites and web applications at the end of the year.
